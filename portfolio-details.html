Rookie Team of the Year Award

We Participated first time in FB 2018. And in the first year itself, we easily clear the TI Test and the Endurance Test, and due to that excellence we got Rookie Team of the Year Award. Rookie Team of the Year Award is given to that team when they participate first time and show their excellent work.


First Team to clear TI Award

Team Nequit 4.0 received First Team to clear TI Award in 2018 which is given to that team who completes the Technical Inspection in first attempt. The award was sponsored by JTEKT.

Formula Imperial 2023

October 2023 marked a thrilling chapter in our racing journey as our team took on the challenge of Formula Imperial hosted by ISIE India at the iconic Buddha International Circuit. Riding high on innovation , we have proudly presented our firsts electric vehicle “Aarambh”, which symbolizes innovation, sustainability, and a promising future.
We completed Electrical and Mechanical Technical Inspection, Brake test and arrived for Endurance Event.
