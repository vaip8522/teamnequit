About Us

“Winners are not people who never fail but the people who Never Quit”. Team Nequit was formed with the same motto in 2014 by the students of Rajarambapu Institute of Technology, Sakharale. The mission was to design, build and manufacture a formula student vehicle with a vision to create an industrial environment and develop engineers following industrial practices which could ultimately help them establish potential professions in the future.

Mission & Vision

Mission:-

To design, build and manufacture a formula student vehicle

Vision:-

To create an industrial environment and develop engineers following industrial practices


Team Values:-

Teamwork.

Our Achievements







Pi-EV Competition is platform which provides an opportunity to conceptualize and design a Formula Student Electric Vehicle Powertrain Package which meets rigorous standard set by the Formula Bharat competition. Our journey through the this competition has been a true reflection of collaboration, resilience, and unwavering dedication. The competition's virtual nature did not deter our team's spirit; instead, it ignited our innovative spark, allowing us to explore novel ideas, overcome challenges, and refine our electric vehicle concept.
